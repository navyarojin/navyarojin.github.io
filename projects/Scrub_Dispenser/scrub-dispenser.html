Non-Contact Scrub Dispensing System

Healthcare • IR Sensing • Internship

Developed as part of my 2nd-year summer internship at BCMCH Thiruvalla under IEEE EMBS. This system uses IR-based non-contact sensing and a peristaltic pump to dispense scrub fluid without contamination, clogging, or expensive electronics. The design includes a custom PCB, 3D-printed enclosure, and screw-mount bottle interface suitable for hospital use.

Key Features

  • Touch-free IR sensing for hygienic dispensing
  • Peristaltic pump to prevent backflow and clogging
  • Low-cost analog electronics (no microcontroller required)
  • Custom comparator-based control PCB
  • 3D-printed enclosure with wall-mount system
  • Designed specifically for clinical scrub fluid bottles
